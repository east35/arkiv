## Intent

What problem are we solving? Who experiences it? Why does it matter? Describe the need in plain language — focus on the human impact, not the implementation.

## User Story

- As a [type of user], I want [goal] so that [reason]
- What does success look like from the user's perspective?
- What's the simplest version that delivers value?

## Requirements

- The system SHALL [primary requirement]
- The system SHALL [secondary requirement]
- The system SHALL NOT [explicit exclusion]

> SHALL = mandatory. SHOULD = preferred. MAY = optional.
> These statements are the traceability anchors SDD agents link implementation tasks back to.

## Design Rationale

What approach are we taking and why? Capture the thinking behind key decisions — alternatives considered, trade-offs made, inspiration or references that shaped the direction.

## Constraints

- What are the boundaries we're working within?
- What's explicitly out of scope?
- Are there technical, timeline, or resource limitations?

## Open Questions

- What don't we know yet?
- What needs more research or testing?
- Where do we need input from others?

Observations, research findings, and evolving thinking go here. This section is a living scratchpad.
